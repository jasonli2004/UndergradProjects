To get calories by food name: access http://XXX.X.X.X:YYYY/calorie/food

To get restrictions by food name: access http://XXX.X.X.X:YYYY/diet/food1,food2,... (at least one food, seperate multiple foods by ',')

Replace 'food' with the food of your choice, 'XXX.X.X.X' with your IP address, and 'YYYY' with your port number

Usage example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/calorie/Garden Herb Ranch Sauce

Usage example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/diet/Garden Herb Ranch Sauce,Tomato
